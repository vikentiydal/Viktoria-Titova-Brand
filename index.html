Main Body

Product Overview: The Viktoria Titova collection offers apparel and accessories that celebrate Ukrainian culture, global travel influences, and musical inspiration. From embroidered blouses and dresses to tailored jackets and scarves, each product is ethically crafted, prioritizing sustainable materials and fair wages.

Technical Specifications & Details:

  • Materials: Sustainable linen, organic cotton, ethically sourced wool.
  • Embroidery & Motifs: Traditional Ukrainian patterns, subtle violin emblems, world-map linings.
  • Fit & Sizing: Standard international sizes (XS–XXL); custom options upon request.
  • Care Instructions: Machine wash cold or hand wash; detailed care guides included.

Formats & Versions:

  • Women’s Collection: Blouses, dresses, and scarves with intricate embroidery.
  • Men’s Collection: Jackets, shirts, and ties with subtle travel-inspired accents.
  • Unisex Capsule: T-shirts, accessories, and limited-edition collaborations.
